NGOWA

Starting at $375

K-12 school performance

N'Gowa is a traditional word from the Baoule tribe in Cote D'Ivoire. Ngowa means: music and dance time, and also commonly known as "play time." In this program, students will learn about African dance, music, and history.

Always we reserve the big surprise at the end

ANOUANZE

Starting at $500

College & University Lecture

Anouanze is a traditional word from the Baoule and Gode tribes in Cote D'Ivoire (Ivory Coast, West Africa). Anouanze means peaceful agreement, speaking from the same heart. Anouanze is a joyful celebration through solidarity. This program brings traditional African masked dancers, drummers, and stilt walker.

Through cultural art and education we can better understand others

AYOKA

Starting at $1000

Community & Corporate program

Ayoka is a traditional word from the Bete and Dida tribes in Cote D'Ivoire. Ayoka means Thank you and also translated as "the one who brings joy. Ayoka is the deepest regard and honest compassion that one brings in times of sorrow, happiness, or grieving. This program offers traditional African masked dancers, drummers, and an acrobat stilt walker;"

"We can lift up someone spirit just by sending words of joy and positive vibe.just by the meaning of our word."
